Gremlin supports typical math operations and relations. This section of the documentation will review these by way of examples.

h2. Addition

bc. gremlin> 1 + 2 
==>3
gremlin> 1.23 + 2 
==>3.23

h2. Subtraction

bc. gremlin> 1 - 2
==>-1
gremlin> 1.23 - 2
==>-0.77

h2. Multiplication

bc. gremlin> 1 * 2
==>2
gremlin> 1.23 * 2
==>2.46

h2. Division

bc. gremlin> 1 div 2
==>0.5
gremlin> 1.23 div 2
==>0.6150000095367432

h2. Modulo

bc. gremlin> 1 mod 2
==>1
gremlin> 1.23 mod 2
==>1

h2. Greater Than, Less Than, Equal To

bc. gremlin> 1 > 2
==>false
gremlin> 1 >= 2
==>false
gremlin> 1 < 2
==>true
gremlin> 1 <= 2
==>true
gremlin> 1 = 2
==>false
gremlin> 1 != 2
==>true
